Privacy policy

AI Visual Generator (Spin-off No. 5 – marketing visual prompt system) is a minimal app. Here is a quick overview of your data.

Do we collect your data?

No. At the moment, we do not collect any personal data. There is no form submission, email capture, or data sent to servers.

What stays on your device?

Only browser localStorage is used: your selected theme, chosen language, last open section, active generator state, and selected tool can be saved locally in your browser. The data stays on your device only.

If a form is added later

If we later add a contact form or similar feature, this privacy policy will be updated clearly to explain what is collected and how it is used.
